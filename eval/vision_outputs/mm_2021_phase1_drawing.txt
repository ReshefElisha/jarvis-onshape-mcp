## UNCERTAINTIES
1. **F5 pocket depth not explicitly dimensioned.** "5 WALL THICKNESS TYP." clearly fixes the tube wall and likely the pocket floor, but the drawing does not give a direct "pocket depth" callout. Best inference: if the plate is 20 mm thick and the pocket floor is 5 mm thick, pocket depth is 15 mm (from bottom face, z = 0, up to z = 15). Downstream agent should assume this unless section A-A contradicts.
2. **Perimeter rim outer shape vs pocket outer shape.** The pocket perimeter appears to follow the outer 80×80 / R75 / R12 outline offset inward by 5 mm — so the visible "rim" on the bottom is a uniform 5 mm wide strip. This is consistent with the bottom view but is partially render-inferred.
3. **Four radial ribs run corner-to-center.** From the bottom view, each corner bolt-hole boss is connected to the central tube boss by a 5 mm thick straight rib. The exact rib width and whether the ribs are tangent to circles or straight is render_inferred; the drawing does not dimension them beyond "5 WALL THICKNESS TYP."
4. **Lug 30° angle interpretation.** Detail B shows 30° as the half-angle between the lug centerline and the tangent line where the lug profile meets the Ø60 tube. I interpreted this as the angle defining where the R12 lug arc tangentially meets the tube's outer cylinder. If instead it is the full included angle (not half), the lug will be narrower than modeled — worth double-checking.
5. **Lug side.** I assumed the clamp lug is on the +Y side based on the FRONT view orientation; the drawing does not mark a specific axis label. This is a rotation-about-Z choice and does not affect geometry.
6. **M8×1.0 vs standard M8×1.25.** Drawing explicitly calls M8×1.0 (fine pitch). Do not substitute the more common M8×1.25.
7. **Order of sawcut vs bolt holes vs fillets.** The Ø9 "THRU TO SAWCUT" language implies the sawcut is made first (or at least that the Ø9 bore terminates at the sawcut plane). Build order should be: lug boss → sawcut → counterbore hole → tapped hole → fillets. Inverting this will change edge geometry around the sawcut/bolt intersection.
